1. Who We Are

The Hair Company ("we", "us", "our") is a hair salon located at Shop Street, Galway City, Ireland. We are the data controller for the personal information collected through this website and in the course of providing our services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Irish Data Protection Acts 1988–2018.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Identity data: first name, last name
  • Contact data: email address, telephone number, postal address
  • Appointment data: service type, stylist preference, date and time of appointment, booking history
  • Health data: allergy information or patch test results (only where necessary for service delivery and with your explicit consent)
  • Marketing data: your preferences for receiving marketing communications
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and other analytics data collected via cookies

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and manage your appointments
  • Send booking confirmations and reminders
  • Communicate with you about your appointments and services
  • Send marketing communications where you have opted in
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to your enquiries and complaints

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract: processing necessary to fulfil your appointment booking
  • Consent: marketing emails, cookies, and sensitive data such as allergies
  • Legitimate interests: improving our services, fraud prevention, and security
  • Legal obligation: compliance with applicable Irish and EU law

5. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Our booking software provider (e.g., Fresha/Phorest) for appointment management
  • Email service providers for sending confirmations and newsletters
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics) — anonymised data only
  • Legal or regulatory authorities where required by law

All third-party providers are required to process your data only in accordance with our instructions and applicable data protection law.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Appointment and client records: 3 years from your last appointment
  • Allergy/patch test records: 5 years (for health and safety compliance)
  • Marketing preferences: until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent
  • Financial records: 7 years (Irish tax law requirement)
  • Website analytics: 26 months

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: ask us to limit how we process your data
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object: object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent: at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@thehaircompany.ie. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission of Ireland.

8. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These include essential cookies (required for the website to function), analytics cookies (to understand how visitors use our site), and marketing cookies (where you have consented). You can manage your cookie preferences via our cookie banner or your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for full details.
