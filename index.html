FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Book online 24/7 through our booking page, call us on 091 123 456 during opening hours, or send us a message on Instagram or Facebook. We recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance for colour services and 2-4 weeks for bridal appointments.
For colour services, we recommend arriving with your hair unwashed for 24-48 hours beforehand. This helps natural oils protect your scalp during processing. Bring reference photos of the look you'd like, and don't worry about removing any existing colour — we'll assess your hair at the consultation. A patch test is required at least 48 hours before if you haven't been coloured by us before.
Absolutely — we always recommend a bridal trial 4-8 weeks before the wedding day. This allows you to test your chosen style, refine any details, and ensure you feel completely confident and beautiful on the big day. We'll note the exact techniques, timings, and products used so your wedding day appointment runs perfectly.
We're conveniently located on Shop Street in the heart of Galway city, just a short walk from Eyre Square. We're easily accessible by bus, and there is paid parking available at the nearby Eyre Square Shopping Centre and Jurys Inn car parks. See our Contact page for a full map and directions.
We kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. For colour, treatment, or bridal appointments we require 48 hours' notice. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a charge of 50% of the service price. We understand emergencies happen — please contact us as soon as possible and we'll always do our best to accommodate you.
We use only the finest professional brands in the industry including Wella Professionals, Olaplex, Kérastase, and GHD. These premium products ensure outstanding results while keeping your hair in the best possible condition. Our retail area stocks a curated selection of these products so you can maintain your salon results at home.
