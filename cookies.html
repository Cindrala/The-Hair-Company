Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how The Hair Company uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website (thehaircompany.ie). By clicking "Accept All" on our cookie banner, you consent to our use of non-essential cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your experience. Some cookies are essential for the website to work; others are optional and used for analytics or marketing purposes.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
thc-cookie-consent Essential Remembers your cookie consent preference 1 year
session_id Essential Maintains your session while browsing the site Session
booking_session Essential Stores booking form data during the booking process Session
_ga Analytics Google Analytics — distinguishes unique users 2 years
_ga_XXXXXX Analytics Google Analytics 4 — maintains session state 2 years
_gid Analytics Google Analytics — distinguishes users within a 24-hour window 24 hours
fbpx Marketing Facebook Pixel — tracks conversions from Facebook ads 90 days

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They include cookies that manage your login session, remember your cookie preferences, and enable core functionality such as the booking system.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website — which pages are most popular, how people navigate the site, and where visitors come from. This helps us improve the website experience. All data is anonymised and aggregated. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Marketing Cookies

If you have consented, we may use marketing cookies (such as the Facebook Pixel) to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns. These cookies do not allow us to personally identify you.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by:

  • Clicking "Manage Cookies" in the footer (when available)
  • Clearing cookies and revisiting our site to see the cookie banner again
  • Adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services embedded in our website (such as Google Maps or booking widgets) may set their own cookies. These are governed by the respective third parties' privacy policies.

More Information

For more information about how we handle your personal data, please read our Privacy Policy. For any cookie-related queries, contact us at hello@thehaircompany.ie.
