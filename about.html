Our Story

Fifteen Years of Galway Hair

Born in Galway, built on passion, and driven by an unwavering commitment to making every client feel extraordinary.

How It All Began

A Galway Dream, Fifteen Years Strong

It started in 2009 with a simple belief: that everyone deserves to walk out of a salon feeling like the best version of themselves. Founder Siobhán Murphy opened The Hair Company's first location on Shop Street with just three chairs, a passionate team, and an unshakeable vision.

What began as a small studio has grown into one of Galway's most beloved and trusted salons. We've been part of thousands of first dates, wedding days, job interviews, and ordinary Tuesdays that became extraordinary — because the right hair can change everything.

Today, our team of 12 specialist stylists bring together decades of combined experience, international training, and a shared love for their craft. We remain proudly rooted in Galway — a city whose creativity, spirit, and warmth is woven into everything we do.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Three principles that guide everything we do — from the products we choose to the way we greet you at the door.

Excellence Without Compromise

We never cut corners. Every appointment — whether a €12 fringe trim or a full colour transformation — receives the same meticulous care and skill. Because to our clients, every appointment matters.

People Before Everything

Hair is personal. We listen first, advise honestly, and always prioritise your hair's health and your happiness over selling services you don't need. Our clients are family — and we treat them as such.

Always Learning

The hair world evolves constantly. Our entire team attends regular advanced training, international shows, and brand education events to ensure we bring the very latest techniques and knowledge to every client.

The People Behind the Magic

Meet Our Team

Talented, passionate, and deeply committed to making you look and feel your absolute best.

Siobhán Murphy

Founder & Colour Director

Siobhán founded The Hair Company in 2009 after training in Dublin, London, and Paris. Her passion for colour artistry has made her one of the most sought-after colourists in Connacht. She specialises in balayage, colour correction, and cut-and-colour transformations.

Declan Walsh

Men's Grooming Specialist

Declan brings 10 years of barbering and salon experience to every cut. A Galway native, he's passionate about modern men's styles and beard artistry. Known for his attention to detail and effortless client rapport, Declan has a loyal following of men who wouldn't trust anyone else with their hair.

Aoife Brennan

Bridal & Extensions Specialist

Aoife has been creating unforgettable bridal looks for over 8 years. Her meticulous approach, artistic eye, and calming presence make her the perfect partner for brides on their most important day. She is also our lead extension specialist, trained in nano ring, tape-in, and micro ring methods.

Conor O'Brien

Conor is our newest senior stylist, bringing a fresh creative energy and an editorial-inspired approach to every service. Trained at the prestigious Toni&Guy Academy in Dublin and further educated in London Fashion Week shows, Conor brings runway energy to every chair.

Our Journey

15 Years in Galway

2009

The Beginning

Siobhán opens The Hair Company on Shop Street with three styling chairs and a dream. Word spreads quickly — the city takes notice.

2012

Expanding the Team

Growing demand means growing the team. We welcome Fionnuala and introduce specialist colour services for the first time.

2015

Salon Expansion

We expand into a larger premises, adding 6 more styling stations and launching our bridal services with the arrival of Aoife.

2018

Awards & Recognition

Named "Best Galway Hair Salon" at the Connacht Business Awards. Our social media presence grows to 10,000+ followers.

2021

Reinvention

After the challenges of the pandemic, we emerged stronger — complete salon refurbishment, online booking launch, and expanded treatment menu.

2024

15 Years & Thriving

We celebrate 15 years and 5,000+ loyal clients. Still passionate, still learning, still proud to call Galway our home.
