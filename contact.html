How to Find Us

Getting to The Hair Company

We're conveniently located in the heart of Galway city — easy to reach by bus, foot, or car.

🚶

On Foot

Just a 5-minute walk from Eyre Square and close to the Latin Quarter and Spanish Arch.

🚌

By Bus

Galway city bus routes stop at Eyre Square — a 5-minute walk to our door. Bus Éireann routes also serve the city centre.

🚗

By Car

Paid parking available at Eyre Square Shopping Centre and Jurys Inn car parks, both a short walk away.
