1. About The Hair Company

The Hair Company is a professional hair salon located at Shop Street, Galway City, Ireland. We provide hair cutting, colouring, styling, treatment, and related services. These terms govern your use of our website (thehaircompany.ie) and any appointments made with us.

2. Booking & Appointments

2.1 Making a Booking

Appointments can be made online via our website, by phone on 091 123 456, or in person at the salon. A booking is only confirmed once you receive a confirmation email or SMS from us.

2.2 Deposits

A deposit may be required to secure appointments for:

  • Colour services (including balayage, highlights, full colour): €25
  • Extension appointments: €50
  • Bridal/wedding hair (trial and wedding day): €50
  • Keratin treatments: €50

Deposits are deducted from your final bill. Deposits are non-refundable in the event of a cancellation within 48 hours of the appointment or a no-show.

2.3 New Clients — Colour Patch Test

All new clients booking colour services are required to complete an allergy/patch test at least 48 hours before their appointment. This is a legal and safety requirement. Failure to complete a patch test may result in us being unable to carry out your colour service.

3. Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

3.1 Standard Services

We require at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule a standard appointment (cuts, blow-dries, men's services). Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged 50% of the service price.

3.2 Specialist Services

For colour, treatments, extensions, and bridal appointments, we require at least 48 hours' notice. Late cancellations or no-shows for these services will result in loss of the deposit and may be charged up to 50% of the service price.

3.3 Exceptional Circumstances

We understand that emergencies happen. Please contact us as early as possible if you need to cancel at short notice and we will always do our best to accommodate you fairly.

4. Late Arrivals

If you arrive more than 15 minutes late for your appointment, we may need to shorten or reschedule your service to avoid impacting other clients. Where a rescheduled appointment results in loss for the salon, the cancellation policy above may apply.

5. Service Satisfaction

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you are unhappy with any aspect of your service, please raise it with your stylist or a manager during or immediately after your appointment. We will always do our best to address any concerns. Rectification services are offered where appropriate at our discretion. Refunds for services already rendered are not generally provided; however, each situation will be assessed individually and fairly.

By booking a colour service, you confirm you have completed or agreed to complete a patch test as required.
